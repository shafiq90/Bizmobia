Privacy Policy

BizMobia shall have the right to modify, update or amend the terms of this Privacy Policy at any time by placing the updated Privacy Policy on the Website. By continuing to interact with BizMobia or by continuing to use BizMobia services following the modifications, updates or amendments to this Privacy Policy shall signify your acceptance of such modifications, updates or amendments.

“Personal Information” refers to personal data which you may provide to BizMobia and any information that can be used to identify you.

“Processing” refers to collecting, holding, storing, recording, organizing, adapting, using, disclosing or correcting Personal Information.

What Personal Information BizMobia collects from you?

BizMobia collects Personal Information from you when you register with BizMobia, when you use services from BizMobia or use the Website such as but not limited to your name, address, telephone number, NRIC/passport number, email address and credit/debit card details. BizMobia also collects Personal Information when you complete any survey done by us. Website usage information may also be collected using cookies.

The provision of your Personal Information is voluntary. However, in the event you do not provide the required Personal Information, e-Wallet may not be able to communicate with you and/or to provide you with the services requested.

Cookies and Google Analytics:

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by websites that you visit. They are widely used in order to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site. This Website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses "cookies", which are text files saved on your computer, to help the Website analyze how you use the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the Website will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. If this Website anonymizes IP addresses, your IP address will be truncated by Google within a EU member state or other EEA state before being transmitted to the US. Only in exceptional situations will your full IP address be transmitted to Google servers in the United States and truncated there. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the Website, compiling reports on Website activity for Website operators and providing other services relating to Website activity and internet usage.

You can also prevent Google from collecting Personal Information (including your IP address) via cookies and processing this Personal Information by downloading this browser plugin and installing it: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

How will BizMobia use the Personal Information collected from you?

Personal Information collected by BizMobia is used to process your order and to manage your account. BizMobia may also use your Personal Information to email you about other products or services that BizMobia thinks may be of interest to you and to comply with its obligations under applicable laws, where required.

As BizMobia’s storage facilities and servers may be located in other jurisdictions, your Personal Information may be transferred to, stored, used and processed in a jurisdiction other than India. From time-to-time BizMobia may send your Personal Information to third parties which BizMobia considers may have goods or services which are of interest to you if you are using the respective services only. If you do not wish to be contacted by third parties, please email to BizMobia Data Compliance Officers at policy@bizmobia.com.

Access to your Personal Information and rectification requests

Subject to any exceptions under applicable laws, you have the right to request a copy of the Personal Information and rectify the Personal Information e-Wallet holds on you at any time. Please email to e-Wallet’s Data Compliance Officer if you would like to receive a copy of this information, to submit a Personal Information rectification request or if you seek further information on this Privacy Policy – policy@bizmobia.com. There will be a small charge for processing this Personal Information access request.

Retention of your Personal Information

BizMobia will retain your Personal Information for the duration necessary to fulfil the foregoing purposes and until you delete your services offered by us.
